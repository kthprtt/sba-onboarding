🔒 Sure Bet — Guaranteed Profit START HERE

Two sportsbooks disagree on the odds for the same game. You bet BOTH sides on different books. One HAS to win. You profit either way. This is not gambling — it's math.

Real Example — NBA: Lakers vs Celtics
Side A: Lakers +150 on DraftKings → Bet $400
Side B: Celtics -130 on FanDuel → Bet $600
Total deployed: $1,000

If Lakers win: $400 × 2.50 = $1,000 + $62 = +$62 profit
If Celtics win: $600 × 1.769 = $1,000 + $62 = +$62 profit

✅ You make $62 NO MATTER WHO WINS. That's 6.2% in one bet.

When to use it: Always. This is your foundation. Every other strategy builds on top of Sure Bets.

Risk:
Zero

Same arb odds, but you load the likely winner. If the favorite wins (which it usually does), you make MORE than a Sure Bet. If the underdog wins, you break even. Zero risk, higher upside.

Same odds: +190 / -110 with $1,000
Sure Bet split: $396.98 / $603.02 → +$151 either way

Value Fav split: $344.83 / $655.17 (load the -110 side)
If favorite wins: +$250.72 (25% ROI)
If underdog wins: $0 (break even)

Same odds. Same two books. 65% more profit when the favorite wins. Zero downside.

When to use it: When the favorite is heavily favored (-200 or more) and you're confident in the likely outcome. The math still protects you either way.

Risk:
Zero — break even is the worst outcome. You never lose money.

🎰 Middle Bets JACKPOT

Two books have different lines on the same prop. You bet Over on one book, Under on the other. If the result lands IN THE MIDDLE, you win BOTH bets. Outside the middle, you still profit (it's still an arb).

Steph Curry Points — Over/Under
Book A: Over 28.5 @ -110 → Bet $500
Book B: Under 30.5 @ -110 → Bet $520

If Curry scores 27: Under wins → small profit (arb)
If Curry scores 29 or 30: 🎰 BOTH WIN! JACKPOT!
If Curry scores 31: Over wins → small profit (arb)

The middle window (29-30) is your jackpot zone. Outside it, you still profit from the arb.
