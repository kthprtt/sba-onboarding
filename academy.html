📈 Value Bets — Same Odds, Bigger Profit
Same arb. Same two books. You just shift WHERE the profit goes. Break even worst case — zero risk.
THE CONCEPT

Instead of splitting money evenly (Sure Bet), you load one side. If the favorite wins, you make 25% instead of 15%. If the underdog wins, you break even. You shifted the profit from "guaranteed small" to "likely bigger, worst case $0."
