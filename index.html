  2. Find an Arb Card — look for cards showing positive ROI (green percentage). Higher ROI = more profit.
  3. Read the Card — each card shows:
    • The event (e.g., Lakers vs Celtics)
    • Side A: what to bet and where (e.g., "Lakers ML @ DraftKings +150")
    • Side B: what to bet and where (e.g., "Celtics ML @ FanDuel -140")
    • The ROI percentage (your guaranteed profit)
  4. Open the Calculator — click the arb card to open the calculator. Enter your total stake amount.
  5. Place Side A — go to the first sportsbook, find the exact bet, and place the amount the calculator tells you
  6. Place Side B — immediately go to the second sportsbook and place the other side
  7. Verify — check that both bets are confirmed. You're now guaranteed profit regardless of outcome! 🎉

• Speed matters — odds can change. Place both sides within 1-2 minutes.
• Check the odds match — before placing, verify the odds on the sportsbook match what Genius shows.
• Start small — your first few bets should be smaller amounts until you're comfortable with the process.
• Don't round — bet the exact amounts the calculator gives you, even if it looks weird ($147.32).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is arbitrage betting legal? ▶
Yes. Arbitrage betting is completely legal. You're simply placing bets at different sportsbooks — there's no law against that. However, some sportsbooks may limit accounts that consistently win through arbitrage, which is why we use multiple books and smart bet sizing.
How much money do I need to start? ▶
We recommend starting with $1,000-3,000 spread across 3-5 sportsbooks. The more capital you have, the more profit per bet. Even with $500 total, you can start seeing results — it'll just be smaller amounts per arb.
How much profit can I expect? ▶
Individual arb bets typically yield 1-5% ROI. With multiple bets per day and a healthy bankroll, members commonly see $50-500+ per day depending on their capital and how many arbs they place. Your mileage varies based on bankroll size and activity level.
What if the odds change before I place both sides? ▶
Speed is key — place both sides within 1-2 minutes. If odds move significantly, the arb may no longer be profitable. SBA Genius shows real-time odds, so you'll see if an arb is still active. If one side has already been placed and the arb disappears, the War Room and Hedge tools help you manage the position.
Will sportsbooks ban me for arbing? ▶
Sportsbooks can limit or restrict accounts, but they rarely outright "ban" you. To minimize detection: vary your bet amounts slightly, don't always max bet, use multiple books so you're not hammering one, and mix in some regular bets. The VPN helps access offshore books that are generally more arb-friendly.
Do I need the VPN? ▶
If you're in a state where most major sportsbooks operate (NJ, NY, PA, MI, etc.), you can arb without a VPN using legal books. The VPN gives you access to offshore sportsbooks (Bovada, BetOnline, MyBookie) which significantly increases your opportunities and often have better odds for arbing.
How does the 40-day cycle work? ▶
Your subscription runs in 40-day cycles starting from your activation date. You'll receive reminders before your cycle ends. To continue uninterrupted access, renew before your cycle expires. If you don't renew, your access pauses until you do.
How do I get paid from the Bookie Program? ▶
Affiliate commissions are tracked in real-time in your affiliate dashboard. Payouts are processed at the end of each cycle via CashApp, Zelle, crypto, or bank transfer. You can see your pending and paid commissions in the Payouts tab.
Who do I contact for help? ▶
Join the SBA Discord — it's the fastest way to get support. You can also email support@sportsbetarbitrage.com. For VPN issues, reach out in the #vpn-support channel on Discord.
