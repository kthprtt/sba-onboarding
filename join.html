Is this gambling? +

No. Arbitrage is mathematics, not gambling. You bet both sides at different sportsbooks where odds create guaranteed profit regardless of outcome. The profit is locked in before you bet.

Is this legal? +

Yes. Sports betting is legal in 38+ US states. Arbitrage is simply placing bets at two different sportsbooks. It's what Wall Street does with stocks, applied to sports.

SBA membership starts at $1,000. For betting bankroll, $500–$2,000 across 2–4 sportsbooks. More bankroll = more profit per arb.

100%. SBA Genius runs in your browser. All sportsbooks have mobile apps. Find arbs on Genius, place bets on the apps, done in 20 minutes.
